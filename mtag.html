Mini Text Adventure Game

this was a game that me and a whole group of people worked on. it is about batteling a principle because he took your phone and you are trying to retrieve it back. the whole game is controlled by buttons. once you defeat someone then a button is available to be pressed while others are disabled. the cool thing that the group added was the random attack. the random attack grabs a random number between 9 and 25 and it does that amount of damage. another thing we added was a dropdown menu for purchasing items. it is useful when in the middle of fights, and finally my special touch was that i added CSS to make it look better, and better fit the theme.

what i learned from this experience was:

1) functions and how to use them

2) learned how to enable and disble buttons

3) learned lots about if statements

4) working and sharing ideas with teammates
