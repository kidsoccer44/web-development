Mini Text Adventure Game

This was a game that me and a whole group of people worked on. It's about battling a principle because he took your phone and you are trying to retrieve it back. The whole game is controlled by buttons. Once you defeat someone then a button is available to be pressed while others are disabled. The cool thing that the group added was the random attack. The random attack grabs a random number between 9 and 25 and it does that amount of damage. Another thing we added was a dropdown menu for purchasing items. It is useful when in the middle of fights, and finally my special touch was that i added CSS to make it look better, and better fit the theme.

What I learned from this experience was:

1) Functions and how to use them

2) Learned how to enable and disble buttons

3) Learned lots about if statements

4) Working and sharing ideas with teammates
