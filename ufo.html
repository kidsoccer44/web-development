UFO Deforsestation

this is a game all about an alien spaceship trying to destroy as many trees has the alien can. all while the U.S. military is trying to destroy you with missiles. this game is calm but it can get crazy if you survive long enough. this game also features to bullet varietes that me and my teammates came up with. the fire will only affect the trees and the bullet bill will only effect the missiles, that's what gives this game more of a challenge. also on of our teamates made it to where the missiles don't randomly spawn in front of you which in turn makes the missile strike the UFO. instead the missiles spawn a out of the camera and slowly come into view.

what i learned from this experience was:

1) Working better among teammates

2) better understanding of variety and including new things

3) better time management
