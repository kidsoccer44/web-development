Copyright

Copyright is a legal right that grants the creator of original work exclusive rights for use and distrubution. This also protects the creators work from others who want to take credit for it and make profit. Although there are some that will just ask to be mentioned when one of their works are being used, or some will ask for payment to include the creators work.

Creative Commons

The creative commons is a place for people can go and use work without having to face the consequenses of copyright. In the creative commons no work is copyrighted and and can be used for personal and work usage. Although there is no copyright you can not take the work and sell it as your own. The creative commons is free to the public.
